Trend 1:
In general, Charter Schools seemed to perform higher than district schools.
Their overall passing rate was higher, however, they also had a lower number of students than the bottom performing schools.

Trend 2: 
When looking at the math and reading scores by grade breakdown, the average scores where higher in reading.
In the district summary, the average reading score and the passing rate where both higher.
This shows that the reading scores tended to be higher when looked in aggregate by district and when broken out by grade and school.
